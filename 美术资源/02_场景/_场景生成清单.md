文档类型: 美术 — 场景生成 prompt 清单
用途: 16 个核心场景的 prompt（先 empty 空场景 → 再合成角色）

# 场景生成清单

> **每个场景先生成 empty 空场景**，验证光线 / 色调 / 家具布置后，**再用 inpaint / regional prompt 把角色"放进去"**。
> **不要一次生成全场景 + 多人**——AI 会把脸混。

---

## 0. 通用模板

```
=== STYLE ANCHOR ===     # 同 _风格指南.md §十
=== LOCATION ===         # 地点详细描述
=== TIME / LIGHT ===     # 时段 + 光线 + 来自风格指南 §四 的光线档案
=== PROPS ===            # 关键道具 + 标志细节
=== COMPOSITION ===      # 镜头 + 角度
=== MOOD ===             # 一句话情绪锚
=== TECH ===             # 比例 + 工具后缀
=== NEGATIVE ===         # 来自风格指南 §六
```

## 1 · S001 · Las Veladas / Avenida del Cobre（铜街凌晨）★★★★★

**剧情位置**:序幕 PROL_C_001——黑色雪佛兰拦下 Saulito 的那条街。
**适配事件**:序幕开场、Acto 中段返乡支线、终章 *Las Veladas* 黎明前。

```
=== STYLE ANCHOR ===
Cinematic photo, shot on 35mm film, Kodak Portra 400 feel,
late 1970s — early 1980s Latin American political documentary aesthetic,
muted earthen palette (umber, copper rust, washed indigo, ochre,
chalk-white stucco), medium film grain, slight halation, slightly
desaturated, warm shadows, painterly weight, references: Roma 2018,
Salgado, Iturbide.

=== LOCATION ===
A narrow rough cobblestone street in a tropical Latin American slum
neighborhood (Las Veladas — "the night-watch district"), cracked stucco
walls in faded ochre and chalk-white peeling colors, low one-story
houses with iron-grilled windows, a small hole-in-the-wall bar
(*El Gallo Borracho*) on the right side with a faded hand-painted
rooster sign and a single bare bulb above the door, distant sounds
of a tropical city implied (not visible).

=== TIME / LIGHT ===
4:17 AM, deep pre-dawn, single yellow tungsten streetlamp from upper
left, wet cobblestones reflecting the lamp, a thin layer of mist at
ground level, no other artificial light, dark indigo sky.

=== PROPS ===
A discarded beer bottle by a doorway, a stray dog barely visible at
the far end of the street (Mariposa), a faded political poster from
years ago peeling on the wall.

=== COMPOSITION ===
Establishing shot, low eye level (street perspective), 16:9 widescreen,
deep one-point perspective down the alley, the bar door positioned
right of center, the wet street leading the eye into darkness.

=== MOOD ===
The street where it begins. Dignity surviving on rum and routine.
The kind of place that does not look up when an unmarked car parks.

=== TECH ===
16:9, 1920×1080.
Midjourney: --ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.1 --s 50

=== NEGATIVE ===
[复制风格指南 §六, 加: not modern / contemporary, not Western European,
not gentrified, not stylized fantasy slum, real working-class Latin
American slum only.]
```

**变体**:
- **S001_est_dusk_v01** — 同地点黄昏版（Acto 中段返回）。
- **S001_est_dawn_v01** — 终章 True Ending 用——黎明前的灰光。
